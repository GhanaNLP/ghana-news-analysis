# Ghana Topics (2025)

Ghana Topics is a collaborative public information project by Ghana NLP to identify key topics that appeared frequently in Ghanaian news annually. The current results are from analyzing over 61,000 news articles published in 2025 across 5 major Ghanaian news outlets. Using NLP techniques and AI-powered classification, we extracted and filtered keywords to surface the topics dominating the news cycle.

------
